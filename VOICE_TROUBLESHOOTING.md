## Common Errors & Fixes

### 1. "Network error. Please check your connection."
**Cause:** 
- You are using **Hindi** voice recognition, which requires an active internet connection.
- Your internet connection is unstable.
- The browser cannot reach Google's speech servers.

**Fix:**
- **Switch to English:** Use the language dropdown at the top of the chat to select "English". English often works offline.
- **Check Internet:** Ensure you have a stable Wi-Fi or data connection.

### 2. "Microphone access denied."
**Cause:**
- You blocked microphone access for this site.
- Your system microphone is muted or disabled.

**Fix:**
- **Allow Access:** Click the lock icon ðŸ”’ in the browser address bar -> Site Settings -> Allow Microphone.
- **Check System:** Go to Windows Settings -> Sound -> Input -> Test your microphone.

### 3. "I didn't hear anything."
**Cause:**
- Background noise.
- Microphone volume too low.
- You stopped speaking for too long.

**Fix:**
- Click the **Refresh Icon (Retry)** button in the center of the screen.
- Speak clearly and closer to the microphone.

## How to Test Voice Mode

1. **Refresh the page** (F5).
2. Select **English** from the language dropdown (for best reliability).
3. Click **"ðŸ’™ Voice Mode"**.
4. If asked, click **"Allow"** for microphone access.
5. Speak "Hello Buddy".
6. You should see your words appear on the screen.

## Still having issues?
If errors persist, try:
- Using **Google Chrome** (it has the best support for Web Speech API).
- Closing other tabs that might be using the microphone.
- Restarting your browser.
